## Quantitative Finance & Forecasting

### [Option-Driven Improvements in Volatility Forecasting](https://nlblom.github.io/assets/volatility-forecasting.pdf)
Incorporated option-implied information into point and density volatility forecasts for the S&P 500, demonstrating out-of-sample gains and improved forecast uncertainty under relaxed distributional assumptions.  
[View project](https://nlblom.github.io/assets/volatility-forecasting.pdf)

### Exchange Rate Density Forecasting using Quantile Regression  
Constructed one-month-ahead conditional density forecasts for USD/EUR returns using two-step quantile regression with PCA and yield-curve factors, achieving significant out-of-sample improvements over a random walk benchmark.  
[View project]([#](https://nlblom.github.io/assets/exchange-rate-forecasting.pdf)

### Comparison of Volatility-Managed Portfolio Models  
Evaluated volatility-managed portfolio strategies using alternative volatility models (including DCC and GARCH) across five Fama-French factors, finding superior risk-adjusted performance and lower skewness relative to benchmark portfolios.  
[View project](https://nlblom.github.io/assets/volatility-managed-portfolios.pdf)


## Strategy & Applied Analysis

### Panel Data Analysis of Health Expenditures and Poverty (Prize-Winning Project)  
Estimated the impact of out-of-pocket health expenditures on poverty across 143 countries (2000–2019) using panel data econometrics, identifying statistically significant effects at conventional poverty thresholds.  
[View project](https://nlblom.github.io/assets/poverty-healthexpenditures.pdf)

### TikTok’s Algorithm and Value-Sensitive Design  
Analysed TikTok’s recommender system through a value-sensitive design framework, evaluating incentive structures, engagement trade-offs, and regulatory implications.  
[View project](https://nlblom.github.io/assets/tiktok-algorithm.pdf)
